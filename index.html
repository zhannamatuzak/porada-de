Who We Are

Porada-de is a free communication platform for Ukrainians in Germany.

We exist purely as a platform for the exchange of information and for giving advice. Please note that we do not provide legal advice. For all details, we strongly advise you to contact the responsible authorities or lawyers.

We act as a project supported by SolidUArity. e.V..
